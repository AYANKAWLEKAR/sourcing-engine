## Purpose
Draft a full Investment Committee Memorandum for a Healthcare IT acquisition. Synthesise all prior DD workstreams into a single institutional-quality IC pack as a PowerPoint presentation. Ends with a hard gate: INVEST / CONDITIONAL INVEST / PASS recommendation with explicit rationale.

---

- "Build the Investment Committee memo"
- "Prepare the IC pack for [company]"
- "Format the IC memo to our house standard"

## Pre-Draft Synthesis

Before writing the first slide, read all four DD reports and the financial model. Identify:

1. The three strongest investment arguments (consistent across all workstreams)
2. The three most significant risks (surfaced in any workstream)
3. Any unresolved open items from any DD workstream
4. The base case MOIC and IRR from the LBO Returns sheet
5. Whether any hard gate was triggered in Financial DD (if yes, this must be reflected in the IC recommendation)

---

## IC Memo Framework

### Investment Thesis (3 pillars maximum)
Each pillar must be:
- One sentence, specific, and supported by data
- Linked to a value creation lever (revenue growth, margin expansion, M&A, or multiple expansion)
- Stress-tested against the bear case

### Risk Framework
For each identified risk:
- State the risk in one sentence
- Quantify the impact on EBITDA or returns where possible
- State the mitigation
- Assign a residual risk rating: Low / Medium / High

### Value Creation Plan
Summarise the 100-day plan and 3-year value creation roadmap:

| Horizon | Workstream | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 0-100 days | Integration, reporting setup, management alignment | Board reporting live, KPI dashboard active |
| Year 1 | Cross-sell, product investment, hiring | Revenue +$X, NRR above 110% |
| Year 2-3 | M&A, payer channel expansion | +1-2 acquisitions, EBITDA margin 30%+ |
| Exit | Strategic / secondary / IPO | 3x+ MOIC, 25%+ IRR |

## Recommendation Logic (Hard Gate)

| Condition | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| No Critical flags, total DD score 75+, base case IRR 20%+ | INVEST |
| No Critical flags, total DD score 60-74, or 1-2 open items | CONDITIONAL INVEST |
| Any Critical flag unresolved, total DD score below 60, or bear case IRR below 15% | PASS |

The recommendation slide must include:
- The recommendation in large bold text (green for INVEST, amber for CONDITIONAL, red for PASS)
- If CONDITIONAL: numbered list of conditions that must be satisfied before investment approval
- If PASS: numbered list of specific reasons
- Base case MOIC and IRR stated explicitly
- Maximum 5 sentences of rationale. No filler language.

---

## Formatting Rules
- Cover slide: full dark navy background (#0D1B2A), white text, Morsebridge Healthcare logo placeholder
- Section divider slides: mid navy (#1B3A5C), white text, section name centred
- Content slides: white background, dark navy headings, accent blue (#2E6DB4) for data
- All financial data in tables — no numbers in bullet points
- Every content slide has a one-line "So What" takeaway in the slide footer
- Arial throughout (cover title 32pt, slide titles 24pt, body 18pt, table text 13pt)
- Confidential footer on every slide: "Confidential | Morsebridge Healthcare | IC Memo | [Company] | [Date]"
- No decorative images or clip art
- Maximum 22 slides including appendices

---

## Constraints
- The IC Memo must be self-contained — a reader who has not seen the DD reports must understand the full picture
- Do not copy-paste slides from DD reports — synthesise and rewrite for IC audience
- Every claim must have a source (CIM, DD report, financial model, or web search)
- If any DD workstream flagged an open item, it must appear in slide 18
- Hard gate logic overrides score — do not recommend INVEST if a Critical flag is unresolved
- Recommendation must be explicit — "we recommend proceeding subject to..." is not acceptable without listing the specific conditions
