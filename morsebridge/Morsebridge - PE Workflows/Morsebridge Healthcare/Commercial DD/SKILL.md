# SKILL.md — Commercial Due Diligence
## Morsebridge Healthcare | Healthcare PE Workspace

---

## Purpose
Run a full Commercial Due Diligence on a Healthcare IT target company. Produce a structured Commercial DD Report as a PowerPoint presentation covering market sizing, competitive positioning, customer analysis, and commercial risks. Output feeds directly into the IC Memo.

---

## Trigger Prompt Examples
- "Run a commercial DD on RevSpring"
- "Draft the commercial DD section of our deal memo"
- "Build the commercial DD report for [company]"

---

## Input Files
- CIM Screening Report in Companies Analyzed/[Company]/CIM Analysis/
- CIM document in Companies Analyzed/[Company]/CIM Analysis/
- Web search for current market data, competitor intel, and news

---

## Research Process

Before drafting, run web searches across the following areas. Minimum 6 searches.

| Research Area | What to Find |
|---|---|
| Market size and growth | TAM, SAM, CAGR, key market reports for the relevant sub-sector |
| Regulatory environment | Current and upcoming regulations affecting the company's market |
| Competitive landscape | Direct and indirect competitors, recent M&A in the space |
| Customer sentiment | KLAS ratings, G2/Gartner reviews, case studies, press coverage |
| Management / company news | Recent hires, product launches, contract wins, awards |
| Comparable transactions | Recent M&A comps in the sub-sector with disclosed multiples |

## Commercial DD Framework

Analyse the target across five dimensions:

### 1. Market Assessment
- TAM, SAM, and SOM sizing with methodology
- Market growth rate and key drivers
- Regulatory tailwinds and headwinds
- Market maturity (emerging / growth / consolidating)
- Morsebridge view on whether the market supports a 3x+ return thesis

### 2. Competitive Positioning
- Competitive landscape map (direct, adjacent, and platform competitors)
- Company's differentiated positioning (product, data, integrations, brand)
- Moat assessment: switching costs, network effects, proprietary data, regulatory certification
- Share of wallet opportunity with existing customers
- Vulnerability to EHR vendor encroachment (Epic, Oracle)

### 3. Customer Analysis
- Customer segmentation (by type, size, geography)
- Revenue concentration analysis (top 5, top 10, top 20 clients)
- Contract length, renewal rates, and NRR trend
- Win/loss dynamics and sales cycle
- Key churn risks and retention drivers

### 4. Commercial Model Assessment
- Go-to-market motion (direct / channel / hybrid)
- Pricing model and ASP trends
- Pipeline quality and coverage ratio (if disclosed)
- Cross-sell and upsell runway
- New logo acquisition efficiency

### 5. Commercial Risk Register
Score each risk 1-5 (1 = minimal, 5 = critical). Include mitigation.

Risks to assess:
- Customer concentration risk
- Competitive displacement risk (EHR vendors, new entrants)
- Regulatory / compliance risk
- Sales execution risk (team, pipeline coverage)
- Pricing pressure risk
- Technology obsolescence risk

## Output: Commercial DD Report (PowerPoint)

### File name: [CompanyName]_Commercial_DD.pptx
### Save to: Companies Analyzed/[CompanyName]/Commercial DD/

### Slide Structure (12-15 slides):

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 | Cover: Company name, "Commercial Due Diligence", date, Morsebridge Healthcare |
| 2 | Executive Summary: 5 key findings, overall commercial score, recommendation |
| 3 | Market Overview: TAM/SAM/SOM waterfall, CAGR, key drivers |
| 4 | Regulatory Environment: current rules, upcoming changes, impact on company |
| 5 | Competitive Landscape: 2x2 matrix (market position vs. product breadth) |
| 6 | Competitive Benchmarking: table comparing company vs. top 3 competitors |
| 7 | Customer Analysis: segmentation chart, concentration table, NRR trend |
| 8 | Contract and Retention Analysis: contract length distribution, renewal rates |
| 9 | Commercial Model: GTM motion, pricing, pipeline metrics |
| 10 | Cross-Sell and Growth Runway: whitespace analysis, upsell opportunity ($M) |
| 11 | Commercial Risk Register: risk table with scores and mitigations |
| 12 | Comparable Transactions: table of M&A comps with EV, multiples, buyer |
| 13 | Commercial DD Score: scorecard table, total score, band |
| 14 | Key Findings and Implications for IC Memo |

## Formatting Rules
- Dark navy (#0D1B2A) title slides, white text
- Content slides: white background, navy headings, accent blue (#2E6DB4) for highlights
- All data in tables or charts — no bullet-only slides
- Arial font throughout (titles 24pt, body 18pt, table text 14pt)
- Every slide has a one-line "So What" takeaway in the footer
- Footer on all slides: "Confidential | Morsebridge Healthcare | Commercial DD | [Company]"
- No decorative images or clip art

---

## Constraints
- Every claim must be sourced (CIM, web search, or stated as "Morsebridge estimate")
- Do not reproduce competitor pricing data without public source
- If NRR is not disclosed, state this explicitly and flag as a due diligence question
- Score with commentary only — no hard pass/fail gate for Commercial DD
- Maximum 15 slides
